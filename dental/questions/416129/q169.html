Question 169 of 518 (Prosthodontics)

A female patient of 53 years of age is in your surgery for the construction of removable partial denture. The patient is in good health and currently being treated for chronic periodontitis. Which of the following denture options are recommended if frequent relining is required ?

Explanation #416129.510 (Scroll down to view)

Relining is the process of adding new base material to the tissue surface of an existing denture in a quantity sufficient to fill the space which exists between the original denture contour and the altered tissue contour.

Acrylic base dentures can be relined.

Metal or cast partial dentures and nylon flexible dentures can not be relined.
